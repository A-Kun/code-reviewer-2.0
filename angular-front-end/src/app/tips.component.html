ProTip: In-place swapping of two numbers.

Python provides an intuitive way to do assignments and swapping in one line. Please refer the below example.


x = 10 y = 20 print(x, y) # 10 20 x, y = y, x print(x, y) # 20 10

The assignment on the right seeds a new tuple. While the left one instantly unpacks that tuple to the names <a> and <b>.
